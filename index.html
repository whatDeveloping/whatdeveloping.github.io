Linen Bedding

Experience a good night sleep with the linen bedding. Made of 100% European flax, the fabric is breathable and gives a feeling of coolness on a hot night and comfort in winter. 100% linen bedding is the perfect choice if you want maximum style and comfort with minimal effort.

Kitchen linen

Kitchen linen made of pure flax, such as aprons and kitchen towels, have been popular for centuries. Eco-friendly, pleasant to touch and practical, they are indispensable both in the kitchen and at the backyard barbecue and will provide comfort and cleanliness as you cook.

Table linen

Tablecloths, placemats, napkins and runners made of pure flax are great choices for special occasions. They create a festive atmosphere, give a sense of authenticity and naturalness, and remind us of those happy times from childhood when the whole family got together at a dinner table to enjoy a meal. We hope that the children in your family will also come to associate a pure flax tablecloth or a runner with a festive event and the warmth of a family atmosphere and will get to experience these wonderful emotions.

Beach linen

Bathe in the sun and feel the breeze coming from the sea on a beautiful summer day on the coast! With beach linen made of 100% natural European flax you will experience a true unity with nature, the warmth of the sand and the whisper of the waves. Hypoallergenic beach linen provides real comfort as it gently touches your skin and quickly absorbs moisture. As you relax on your linen beach towel, you will realize that life indeed is beautiful!

Bath linen

Bath linen LinenVibe consists of top-quality 100% flax bath towels and bath mats, pre-washed to preserve the size and quality. They will pleasantly massage your skin as you step out of the shower or bath. Their ability to absorb moisture and dry quickly will make them your bathroom favorites.
